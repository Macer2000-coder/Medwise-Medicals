About Generic Aadhaar

Generic Aadhaar is a pioneering pharmaceutical initiative founded in 2018 by entrepreneur Arjun Deshpande. With support from Ratan Tata, it aims to make high-quality generic medicines accessible and affordable across India. By sourcing directly from WHO-GMP certified manufacturers, the company enables medicine prices to be reduced by up to 80%.

Benefits for Our Customers

  • Affordable Healthcare: Medicines are offered at much lower prices without compromising on quality.
  • Quality Assurance: All products are from WHO-GMP certified facilities to ensure safety and effectiveness.
  • Wide Range: Access to over 1,000 generic medicines across various health categories.
  • Community Service: Helping local people get the treatment they need at prices they can afford.

Our Commitment

As a proud Generic Aadhaar franchise, we are dedicated to providing affordable and reliable medicines to our community. Our mission is to improve access to healthcare and ease the financial burden on families.
